## Researchers

### [Lamia Hadrich Belguith](https://sites.google.com/site/lamiahadrich/)
Professor, directs the ANLP Research Group (MIRACL, Univ. of Sfax) — the most productive academic group on Tunisian NLP. Senior author on much of the morphology, CODA, STAC, POS, and treebank work.

### [Inès Zribi](https://sites.google.com/site/ineszribi/)
ANLP-RG, MIRACL, Univ. of Sfax. Foundational Tunisian morphology: morphological analysis and disambiguation, CODA-TUN orthography, the STAC spoken corpus, sentence-boundary detection.

### [Abir Masmoudi](https://sites.google.com/site/masmoudiabir/)
MIRACL, Univ. of Sfax. Tunisian ASR resources: TARIC railway corpus and phonetic dictionary; Arabizi transliteration.

### [Fethi Bougares](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fethi-bougares/)
Elyadata (CSO); formerly LIUM, Le Mans University. Prolific in Tunisian speech and NLP: TSAC sentiment corpus, TunArTTS, TEDxTN, TARIC-SLU, SLURP-TN, sub-dialect ID. [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/people/fethi-bougares/).

### [Salima Mdhaffar](https://aclanthology.org/people/salima-mdhaffar/)
LIA, Avignon. TARIC-SLU, SLURP-TN, TEDxTN, speech-encoder analyses for Tunisian.

### [Hatem Haddad](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=l_KmkVcAAAAJ&hl=en)
iCompass co-founder/CTO; RIADI Lab, Univ. of Manouba. TunBERT, TUNIZI, T-HSAB, Tunisian sentiment. [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/people/hatem-haddad/).

### [Chayma Fourati](https://aclanthology.org/people/chayma-fourati/)
iCompass. Co-author of TUNIZI, TunBERT, Tunisian word representations.

### [Salah Zaiem](https://huggingface.co/SalahZa)
Télécom Paris. Led TunSwitch code-switched Tunisian ASR and released open TN/FR/EN ASR models.

### [Wajdi Ghezaiel](https://huggingface.co/wghezaiel)
LINAGORA. Lead author of the Labess Tunisian Derja LLM and its SFT/DPO training stack, and of the Tunisian_Derja_Dataset and TunisianMMLU.

### [Khaled Bouzaiene](https://huggingface.co/khaled123)
Tunisia.AI. Built large Tunisian↔English and synthetic Tunisian instruction/translation datasets (Tunisian_Dialectic_English_Derja and others).

### [Elisa Gugliotta](https://aclanthology.org/people/elisa-gugliotta/)
Univ. Grenoble Alpes / Sapienza. Creator (with Marco Dinarelli) of TArC, the Tunisian Arabish corpus and its multi-task annotation tool.

### [Nadia Ben Mohamed Karmani](https://github.com/NadiaBMKarmani)
REGIM, ENIS Sfax. Built the Tunisian aeb-WordNet and an intelligent Tunisian morphological analyzer.

### [Rahma Boujelbane](https://aclanthology.org/people/rahma-boujelbane/)
MIRACL, Sfax. Tunisian↔MSA bilingual lexicons, POS tagging of spoken Tunisian, MT parallel resources.

### [Saméh Kchaou](https://aclanthology.org/people/s/sameh-kchaou/)
Tunisian sub-dialect identification (Tunis/Sfax/Sousse/Tataouine); Tunisian↔MSA MT.

### Karen McNeil and Miled Faiza
Creators of the Tunisian Arabic Corpus (tunisiya.org) and a Tunisian morphological parser; McNeil also published a large Tunisian Arabic dictionary.

### [Muhammad Abdul-Mageed](https://mageed.arts.ubc.ca/)
UBC, Deep Learning & NLP Lab. Organizes the NADI shared tasks (which include Tunisia) and leads pan-Arabic dialect models (ARBERT/MARBERT). Not a Tunisian-only researcher, but central to the shared-task infrastructure Tunisian work uses.

### [Arij Riabi](https://arijrb.github.io/)
Inria ALMAnaCH, Paris. North-African Arabizi (NArabizi) parsing/POS and code-switching — the Algerian NArabizi treebank used to bootstrap the Tunisian UD treebank.

### [Salima Harrat](https://hal.science/hal-01873779v1)
ENS Bouzaréah (Algeria). Lead author of the Maghrebi Arabic dialect processing survey (covers Tunisian); Maghrebi MT.

## Labs

### [ANLP Research Group (ANLP-RG), MIRACL Lab, Univ. of Sfax](https://sites.google.com/site/anlprg/)
The single most productive academic group on Tunisian NLP — morphology, CODA, STAC, POS, NER, sentiment, treebank. [Corpora page](https://sites.google.com/site/anlprg/corpora-corpus).

### [REGIM Lab, ENIS, Univ. of Sfax](http://www.regim.org/)
Signal/image plus some Tunisian speech and language work; home of the aeb-WordNet effort.

### [LIA, Avignon Université](https://lia.univ-avignon.fr/)
Speech processing; via Estève/Mdhaffar, a major contributor to recent Tunisian speech corpora and systems.

### [UBC Deep Learning & NLP Lab](https://mageed.arts.ubc.ca/)
Hosts NADI shared-task data and pan-Arabic dialect models.

## Companies and organizations

### iCompass
Tunis NLP startup (founded 2019); co-founders Ahmed Nouisser (CEO) and Hatem Haddad (CTO). Released TunBERT and TUNIZI; builds Tunisian/African-dialect chatbots, sentiment, and speech. [GitHub](https://github.com/iCompass-ai).

### [InstaDeep](https://instadeep.com/)
Founded 2014 in Tunis (HQ London; acquired by BioNTech, 2023). Partnered with iCompass to open-source TunBERT.

### [Elyadata](https://github.com/elyadata)
Tunisian/Paris speech-NLP company (Fethi Bougares). Released TunArTTS, TARIC-SLU, TEDxTN, SLURP-TN. [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/Elyadata).

### [Ramitechs](http://www.ramitechs.com/)
Tunisian company that builds and annotates corpora and lexicons using expert linguists (commercial data/annotation vendor).

### [LINAGORA Labs](https://huggingface.co/linagora)
Open-source software company; the LinTO project released large open Tunisian ASR datasets and models, plus the Labess Tunisian Derja LLM, TunisianMMLU, and the FineWeb2 Tunisian split.

### [Tunisia.AI (tunis-ai)](https://huggingface.co/tunis-ai)
Community/organization curating Tunisian models and datasets on HuggingFace (TunSwitch mirror, MADAR-TUN, Derja↔MSA parallel corpora, the TunisianEncodersArena leaderboard).

### [ESPRIT School of Engineering](https://huggingface.co/ESPRIT-Group)
Tunis engineering school; released the ESPRIT-Derja instruct LLMs (Qwen3-based) and the ESPRIT-Derja-Instruct dataset (Mourad Zerai).

### AI4D African Language Program / Knowledge 4 All Foundation
Funder/program behind the [Tunisian Arabizi database project](https://k4all.org/project/database-tunisian-arabizi/) that produced TUNIZI (with iCompass: Chayma Fourati, Hatem Haddad, Malek Naski).

## Tools and toolkits

General Arabic toolkits with **no dedicated Tunisian model**, but routinely used on Tunisian text via MSA/Egyptian models:

- [CAMeL Tools](https://github.com/CAMeL-Lab/camel_tools) — Python toolkit (preprocessing, morphology, dialect ID, NER, sentiment). Databases are MSA and Egyptian.
- [Camelira](https://camelira.camel-lab.com/) — web morphological disambiguator (MSA, Egyptian, Gulf, Levantine — no Tunisian).
- [Farasa](https://farasa.qcri.org/) — segmenter/POS/diacritizer/NER; general Arabic. Python port: [farasapy](https://github.com/MagedSaeed/farasapy).
- [ADIDA](https://adida.abudhabi.nyu.edu/) — web dialect-ID demo covering 25 cities including Tunis and Sfax (MADAR system).
- [ADAM](https://github.com/WaelSalloum/adam) — rule-based dialectal morphological analyzer (Levantine/Egyptian/Iraqi/Gulf); adapted in Tunisian work.

Tunisian-specific tools are listed with their datasets in [README.md](README.md) (TArC multi-task tool, Karmani analyzer/lexicon) and [MODELS.md](MODELS.md) (TunBERT).
